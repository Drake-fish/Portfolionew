Narwhal

Node.js + Express.js + React.js

Narwhal was built as a hackathon project at Bazaarvoice. It won best tool award. This is an internal tool used by support to see exactly what reviews should be showing on clients site, where the reviews are syndicating from, and if any reviews are part of a product family. It elimiates the use of 4 other tools and combines them into one tool. Additonally, it allows users to see what reviews are blocked from syndicating from a retailer or brand, and why. This significantly cuts down on troubleshooting time, and allows the team to be more efficent.
